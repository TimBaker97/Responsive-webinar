Chemistry isn't just about burning isolated compounds to produce colourful flames! Nano-scale machines based upon real-world examples such as the DNA Helicase enzyme have given us a whole host of synthetic targets. This talk will expose you to compounds known as Molecular Tweezers and will touch upon a frontier green synthetic approach towards them
